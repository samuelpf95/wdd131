Project Site Plan

Site Name:

World of Chess

This name was chosen because the website will provide news about major chess players and matches from around the world for every chess enthusiast, regardless of their location.

Optional domain: chess-world-news.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to deliver the most relevant chess updates from across the globe, showing that chess remains a significant and vibrant community. Many people dedicate countless hours to playing and watching chess, even if it's not always visible in everyday life.

Scenarios

  • Who is the current world Champion?
  • When is the next big tournament?
  • Who are the top 10 players right now?
  • Where can I play Chess?
